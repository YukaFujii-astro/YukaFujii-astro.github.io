Contact: yuka.fujii_at_nao.ac.jp

Research

My research goal is to understand the properties of small extrasolar planets (exoplanets), and putting Earth and Solar System into a context.
A particular interest is in studying the surface environment of Earth-like planets. Characterizing these small planets is not straightforward, because their faint signal is easily buried in its orders-of-magnitude brighter host star.
We explore observable signatures of exoplanet atmospheres/surfaces that provide clues to the nature of exoplanets, by modeling the atmospheric structures, simulating the spectral/photometric signatures, and evaluating the detectability.
