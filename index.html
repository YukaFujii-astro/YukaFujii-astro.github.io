Last Modified: March 30, 2018

Welcome to my website. I am an associate professor based at Division of Science, National Astronomical Observatory of Japan.
I am also affiliated with Earth-Life Science Institute, Tokyo Institute of Technology.

Main research interest: exoplanet characterization, planetary atmospheres, planet formation, early Earth condition, and origin of Life.

NOTE: I am on maternity leave from October 2021 to March 2022. I am checking emails, but the response may be delayed.
